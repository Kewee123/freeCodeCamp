How to make coffee
Why make coffee at home? What supplies do you need? Step 1. Grind Step 2. Brew Step 3. Enjoy
Why make coffee at home?

It is delicious and cheaper than buying from the store!

It can be stress relieving and a good way to start your morning

as in a morning routine

I love making coffee at home!
What supplies do you need?

Any hot water boiler will do, coffee beans, and a french press

You don't need a fancy boiler!
  • Here are different coffee roasts
  • Blond
  • Light
  • Medium
  • Dark
Step 1. Grind

Either buy preground or buy whole coffee beans

Grind on medium settings!

Any grinder will do!
Step 2. Brew

Set water boiler to 200 degrees Fahrenheit

Put 30g of coffee into the french press

Put 450g of water into the french press, stir and wait 4 minutes, after 4 minutes stir again and scoop out all the foam at the top

The brew ratio is the amount of coffee divided by the amount of hot water! It is best to start off at a brew ratio of 15 and then experiment!
Step 3. Enjoy

Wait another 5 minutes, then pour and enjoy!

Repeat the process!
